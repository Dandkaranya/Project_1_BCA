One Piece

Welcome to the Movie Wiki page! Here, you'll find information about the epic anime series "One Piece."

Plot Summary

"One Piece" follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew in their quest to find the legendary treasure known as One Piece. Luffy, who gains the ability to stretch like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit, sets out to become the Pirate King and discover the secrets of the world.

Main Characters

  • Monkey D. Luffy: Protagonist and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates.
  • Roronoa Zoro: Swordsman and Luffy's first crew member.
  • Nami: Navigator with exceptional cartography skills.

Writers and Creators

  • Original Manga: Eiichiro Oda
  • Director: Konosuke Uda (initial episodes)
  • Production Studio: Toei Animation

Release Date

"One Piece" first aired on October 20, 1999.
