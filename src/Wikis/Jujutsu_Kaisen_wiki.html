Jujutsu Kaisen

Welcome to the Movie Wiki page! Here, you'll find information about the thrilling anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen."

Plot Summary

"Jujutsu Kaisen" follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities. When he encounters a cursed talisman, his life takes a dark turn as he becomes involved in the dangerous world of curses and sorcery.

Main Characters

  • Yuji Itadori: Protagonist with exceptional physical abilities.
  • Megumi Fushiguro: Yuji's classmate and a skilled jujutsu sorcerer.
  • Satoru Gojo: Powerful jujutsu sorcerer and mentor to Yuji.

Writers and Creators

  • Original Manga: Gege Akutami
  • Director: Sunghoo Park
  • Production Studio: MAPPA

Release Date

"Jujutsu Kaisen" first aired on October 3, 2020.
